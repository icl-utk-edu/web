4th HPCG Performance List Released
New HPCG performance results were announced at the SC15 conference in Austin, TX

During the HPCG BOF the presenters at the BOF were:

  1. Jack Dongarra, Michael Heroux, Piotr Luszczek, HPCG Update: SC'15
  2. Jongsoo Park, Intel HPCG on Intel Architecture Update Nov 2015
  3. Everett Philips, NVIDIA, Update on the CUDA Implementation of the HPCG Benchmark
  4. Bob Ciotti, NASA, HPCG/HPL on Pleiades
  5. Costas Bekas, IBM
  6. Kiyoshi Kumahata, RIKEN, HPCG Performance Improvement on the K computer
  7. Yutong Lu, NUDT, Optimizing HPCG on Tianhe 2 at the Full System Scale

The BOF took place on November 18 from 1:30pm to 3pm CDT in Room 15

The deadline for result submissions was October 31, 2015.
