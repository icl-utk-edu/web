New HPCG performance results announced at the ISC16 conference in Frankfurt, Germany
HPCG Performance List announced at the ISC16

The new HPCG Performance List was announced at the ISC16 conference in Frankfurt, Germany.

The deadline for submissions was May 31, 2016.

The announcement was made during a session entitled The HPCG Benchmark: Challenges It Presents to Current & Future Systems took place on Tuesday, June 21, 2016 in Panaroma 2 Room in Messe Frankfurt.

The full list of June 2016 results is available. Final set of slides presented at the session is also available.
