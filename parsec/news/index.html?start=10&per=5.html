PaRSEC at SC'12
2012-11-09

The PaRSEC team will be represented at SC'12. George Bosilca will give an invited talk at the SCALA workshop on Sunday November 11th at 9AM, and will co-present a talk with Piotr Luszczek on "The many-task revolution: alternative programming paradigms for novel architectures" at the INRIA Booth, on Tuesday November 13th, at 3pm.

The DPLASMA / PaRSEC SC'12 handouts are now available for download.
PaRSEC 1.0.0rc1 released
2012-11-07

The PaRSEC / DPLASMA team is pleased to announce the release of PaRSEC version 1.0.0-rc1. This release candidate is the first in the row and includes the PaRSEC software as well as the DPLASMA dense linear algebra layer. We strongly recomend that all users of previous versions upgrade to 1.0.0-rc1 as soon as possible.
DAGuE merged with PaRSEC
2012-11-07

The DAGuE project has been renamed in PaRSEC, in the process of broadening its use, to new ways of expressing the DAG, facilitate interactions with other task-based, and non-task-based systems, and explore new hardware and architectures.
